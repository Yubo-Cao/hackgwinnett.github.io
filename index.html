Hack-a-What?

HackGwinnett hosts annual hackathons at GSMST for high school students all over Metro Atlanta. A hackathon is a competition for students to gather and create innovative solutions to real-world problems within a certain time constraint. Along with the hackathon, HackGwinnett features mentors, workshops, prizes, and strives to build an inclusive environment for individuals interested in technology. Sign up to attend HackGwinnett's first hackathon in October to build awesome projects, participate in enriching workshops, and make new friends!
